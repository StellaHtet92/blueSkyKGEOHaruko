WELCOME TO KGEO

The KMUTT Geospatial Engineering and InnOvation Center (KGEO) is a new source for geospatial research and development. KGEO is an independent center under the Sciencetific Instrument Center for Standard and Industry, Faculty of Science dedicated to helping Thai government organizations, businesses and society to realize the full benefits of geospatial knowledge by designing, building and deploying robust solutions to real world problems.
